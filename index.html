About the Foundation

Small actions, thoughtfully organized, create lasting change.

Inspired by the belief that humanity is our first religion, NayePankh Foundation turns compassion into structured action. The team mobilizes volunteers, donors, and partners around urgent local needs and long-term upliftment.
