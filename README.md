# 🌱 NayePankh Foundation Website

A modern, responsive NGO website built for **NayePankh Foundation**, a youth-powered nonprofit organization dedicated to education, food relief, women empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

![NayePankh Foundation](https://img.shields.io/badge/NGO-Community%20Impact-green)
![Responsive](https://img.shields.io/badge/Responsive-Yes-blue)
![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow)


---

## 📖 Overview

NayePankh Foundation believes that *every dream deserves a new wing*. This website serves as the digital presence of the organization, showcasing its mission, impact, programs, projects, volunteer opportunities, and donation initiatives.

The platform is designed to provide a seamless user experience across all devices while maintaining accessibility, performance, and modern design standards.

---

### 🏠 Home Page
